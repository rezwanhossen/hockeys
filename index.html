Program Sections

We are an inclusive hockey club that welcomes all who want to play
hockey and be part of a thriving local sports club.

Junior Program

We welcome new players of any skill level, offer excellent coaching and
regularly hold junior development opportunities.

Teenager Program

Players will learn the basics such as dribbling, passing, working with teammates,
and having fun! No experience is required to sign up, most of
our youth players are new to playing!

Professional Program

Boulder Hockey Club takes pride in offering a hockey option for all
levels of players in both Co-Ed and Girls Only teams. This includes options for
those that want to travel more as well as those that prefer to play locally.
