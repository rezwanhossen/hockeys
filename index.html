What is a level hockey?

The highest level of competition, also called "AAA", following the Canadian system.


Why is called hockey?

The name hockey—as the organized game came to be known—has been attributed to the French word hoquet (shepherd's stick).


Why is hockey so physical?

The high intensity bursts require the hockey player to develop muscle strength, power, and anaerobic endurance.


How many types of hockey are there?

In order to help make the distinction between these various games, the word hockey is often preceded by another word i.e. field hockey, ice hockey, roller hockey, rink hockey, or floor hockey.


How many countries play hockey?

The game of field hockey is played widely across the world. Field hockey is, in fact, the second largest team sport in the world played in over 100 countries.


Where is hockey popular?

Ice hockey is immensely popular in Canada, where it is the national winter sport and arguably the country's most popular game.
